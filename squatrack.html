This PASYOU SR30 squat rack boasts superior sturdiness and safety features, making it ideal for your home gym. The heavy-duty steel frame and multiple safety features ensure a stable workout environment. Features like screw-locked anti-slip pads and multiple J-hook and bumper positions provide secure placements for barbells at various heights. This versatile rack allows for squats, bench press, and various other exercises. Additional features include anti-slip floor pads and barbell plate storage columns, making it a well-equipped and user-friendly option for your home gym needs. The PASYOU SR30 comes with a 1-year warranty and reliable customer support for your peace of mind.
