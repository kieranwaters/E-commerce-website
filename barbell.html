Olympic barbell

The Mirafit M3 Hybrid is a 7ft, 20kg Olympic barbell designed for weightlifting exercises, powerlifting, and general gym use. It's made from sprung steel with a black zinc coated center and hard chrome sleeves for durability. The barbell features a hybrid grip pattern with dual knurled marks for hand positioning and no knurling in the center for squats. It's suitable for use with standard 2" Olympic weight plates but not certified for competitions. This barbell boasts a weight capacity of 680kg (1500lb) and a tensile strength of 218k psi. Other features include bronze bushings, eight-piece needle bearings, and branded rubber inserts with end caps.
